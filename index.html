Most Visited Places in Bali

Nusa Penida

Nusa Penida is an island that is part of the Republic of Indonesia which is located southeast of Bali which is separated by the Badung Strait. Near this island there are also other small islands, namely Nusa Ceningan and Nusa Lembongan. You can enjoy the breathtaking view here once in a while while in summer for most beautiful scenery

Pura Lempuyang

Lempuyang Temple, also known as Lempuyang Luhur Temple or Penaratan Agung Lempuyang Temple is one of the largest and most important temples for Hindus to worship on the island of Bali. This temple has been viral since last year, because the towering gate of the temple faces directly to Mount Agung. So many people visited here

Labuhan Kebo

Labuhan Kebo. A name that may not sound very Balinese but this waterfall is so beautiful it is not inferior to many other waterfalls in Buleleng. Not the type that has many fans, the rope is actually quite happy because that way it will always be awake. Passing the Gobleg area, even the entrance to the waterfall is so neat. If you bring a car, it fits really well

Most Favorite Food in Bali

Montana Cafe

The trip to the other side of Bali is long and a refreshment stop is always welcome, yet pickings are slim as you get further from the tourist centres. Montana Cafe Bali, one of the sensational cafes in Kintamani, is a great way to break up the trip, stretch your legs, enjoy sensational views, delicious local coffee and rarified atmosphere of Kintamani.

Melali

There is something about hotels that make you hungry for things you might not normally eat. Perhaps it's being so far away from your own kitchen? Regardless, holidays are for indulgence. What could be more sinful than a plate of freshly made waffles, tumbled with fresh fruit and then smothered in chocolate sauce? I feel better already.

El Kabron

Perched 50 minutes above the sea, on the Uluwatu cliffs, El Kabron offers one of Bali's most secluded sunsets, and so much more. With the expansion finished last year, there is now so much more to love. The white terraces surrounding the sparkling blue pool are the perfect setting for ordering up tapas dishes, fresh seafood and more.
