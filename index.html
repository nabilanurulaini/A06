Visited Places in Bali

Recomendation for places you can go for holiday

Place

Nusa Penida

Nusa Penida is an island that is part of the Republic of Indonesia which is located southeast of Bali which is separated by the Badung Strait. Near this island there are also other small islands, namely Nusa Ceningan and Nusa Lembongan.

Place

Pura Lempuyang

Lempuyang Temple, also known as Lempuyang Luhur Temple or Penaratan Agung Lempuyang Temple is one of the largest and most important temples for Hindus to worship on the island of Bali. This temple has been viral since last year, because the towering gate of the temple faces directly to Mount Agung. So many people visited

Place

Labuhan Kebo

Labuhan Kebo. A name that may not sound very Balinese but this waterfall is so beautiful it is not inferior to many other waterfalls in Buleleng. Not the type that has many fans, the rope is actually quite happy because that way it will always be awake. Passing the Gobleg area, even the entrance to the waterfall is so neat. If you bring a car, it fits really well

Favorite Cafe in Bali

Recomendation restaurant/cafe to eat

Restaurant/Cafe

Montana Del Cafe

The trip to the other side of Bali is long and a refreshment stop is always welcome, yet pickings are slim as you get further from the tourist centres. Montana Cafe Bali, one of the sensational cafes in Kintamani, is a great way to break up the trip, enjoy sensational views, delicious local coffee and rarified; atmosphere of Kintamani.

Restaurant/Cafe

Melali Cafe

There is something about hotels that make you hungry for things you might not normally eat. Perhaps it's being so far away from your own kitchen? Regardless, holidays are for indulgence. What could be more sinful than a plate of freshly made waffles, tumbled with fresh fruit and then smothered in chocolate sauce? I feel better already.

Restaurant/Cafe

El Kabron

Perched 50 minutes above the sea, on the Uluwatu cliffs, El Kabron offers one of Bali's most secluded sunsets, and so much more. With the expansion finished last year, there is now so much more to love. The white terraces surrounding the sparkling blue pool are the perfect setting for ordering up tapas dishes, fresh seafood and more.

Popular Hotels in Bali

Recomendation for places you can go for holiday

Hotel

Kumbuh Jungle

Kumbuh Jungle is snuggly nestled in the lush jungle of Bawak river at Kumbuh Village. Designed by local talented workers, our bamboo house will create serene space immersed with Balinese Taksu and life quintessence, let Kumbuh Jungle be your space to clear mind and spirit.

Hotel

Wapa at Ume Sideman

This boutique bolthole in the 'New Ubud' reclines in a rich sweep of rice paddies on the lower slopes of Mount Agung. Private pools star alongside a glorious two-tier infinity pool, and there's a superb spa and tempting restaurant, too.

Hotel

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Sayan

Rising out of rice fields above the Ayung River, the Four Seasons Sayan is a sophisticated tropical resort offering swish facilities, sterling service and experiences to remember, from biking through local villages to art tours and beautiful Balinese healing rituals
